Oberyn Martell

About Oberyn

Prince Oberyn Nymeros Martell of Dorne, also known as the Red Viper, is a member of House Martell and is the younger brother of Doran Martell, Prince of Dorne. Oberyn has eight bastard daughters called the Sand Snakes, the four youngest of whom are by his current paramour, Ellaria Sand.

Oberyn's characteristics

  1. He's tall
  2. He's Slender
  3. He's Graceful
  4. He's a fierce warrior especially in hand to hand combat
  5. He's witty and sharp of tongue
